How it works

Swap with SushiSwap in three steps

SushiSwap keeps the trade flow direct: connect a wallet, choose the tokens and network, then review the live route before any transaction leaves your wallet.

Step 01

Connect your wallet

Open the Sushi app and connect an onchain wallet. You keep control of your keys and approve each action yourself.

Step 02

Pick tokens and chain

Select the asset you want to sell, the asset you want to receive, and the chain where the trade should happen. The app surfaces the route and quote for review.

Step 03

Review and confirm

Check the live quote, price impact, slippage settings, and gas cost, then sign the swap from your wallet when the route matches your intent.

Why SushiSwap

A sharper DeFi swap experience

Self-custody stays first

SushiSwap is built for wallet-based trading. You connect through a wallet such as MetaMask, approve the transaction, and keep custody throughout.

Tokens and chains in one flow

Use SushiSwap for DeFi assets, stablecoins like USDC, and popular onchain markets across supported networks from a consistent interface.

Quotes before signatures

See the live route, expected output, slippage controls, and network fee context before confirming. Final execution depends on the market and chain state at signing.

Got questions?

SushiSwap FAQ

The essentials on using SushiSwap for non-custodial swaps, token routes, fees, timing, assets, and safety.

Open App
01

What is SushiSwap?

SushiSwap is a non-custodial DeFi app for token swaps and liquidity access. It uses smart contracts so wallet users can trade onchain without handing assets to a centralized exchange.

02

Is SushiSwap non-custodial?

Yes. You keep your private keys and sign transactions from your wallet. SushiSwap does not require an exchange account to preview a route.

03

Which chains and assets are supported?

SushiSwap focuses on major DeFi assets, stablecoins, and supported onchain networks. See the full list on the supported chains page before choosing a route.

04

How long does a SushiSwap trade take?

Timing depends on the selected network, wallet confirmation, and current block conditions. The app shows live transaction context before you sign.

05

What does it cost to use SushiSwap?

Costs depend on the route, token pair, liquidity, and the underlying network gas. Review the quote and fee details in the app before confirming.
